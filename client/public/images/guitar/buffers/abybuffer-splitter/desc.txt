Description
A very powerful pedals for working musicians who want to be more present in the mix of performances and have a high level of control over the course of their guitar signal in the chain. It is a pedal that splits the guitar signal as needed to two amps or pedalboards simultaneously by empowering it with the help of two integrated buffer circuits in order to avoid any losses in the strength and color of the basic sound of the guitar.

Pedal is equipped with two switches, you can choose whether the signal will flow to A side, B side or to both sides simultaneously. The device is very suitable for stereo setup.
