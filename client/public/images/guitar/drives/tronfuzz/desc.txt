Description
Alexander FX Tron Fuzz is a versatile silicon fuzz pedal that has the following controls:

– Fuzz
– Tone
– Volume
– PW (Pulse Width)
– Release/Push (controls the input signal strength)
– Subs (controls the output of bass frequencies in order to better adapt the device to different types of instruments)

This device is based on Zvex Mastotron fuzz. It is possible to further improve the relay bypass system.
